## Purpose
A functional 3D office simulator where AI agents work, interact, and collaborate in real-time. Inspired by "Dale & Dawson Stationery Supplies" and "Claw3D".

## Core Objective
Provide an open-source, visually immersive platform for observing and managing multiple autonomous AI agents in a shared spatial environment.

## Target Audience
- AI Developers and researchers.
- Open-source enthusiasts.
- Fans of "autonomous agent" simulations and games.

## Key Use Cases
- Observing multi-agent interactions in 3D.
- Real-time agent status monitoring (Thoughts, Speech, Actions).
- Experimenting with autonomous agent behaviors in an office setting.

## Success Metrics
- Functional 3D environment with interactive agents.
- Multi-agent collaboration through shared world state.
- Seamless OpenRouter integration for agent "brains".
